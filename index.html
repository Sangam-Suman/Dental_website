About the Doctor

Dr. Rakesh Kumar

MDS (Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery) · Trauma Specialist · Maxillofacial Surgeon

Dr. Rakesh Kumar is a highly experienced Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon with over a decade of expertise in treating complex facial trauma, jaw disorders, and reconstructive surgeries. He completed his MDS from a premier dental institution and has since dedicated his career to providing modern, patient-centric care.

His specialization in trauma management makes him one of the most sought-after surgeons for fractured jaws, facial injuries, and deformity corrections. He is committed to using the latest surgical techniques to restore both function and aesthetics for his patients.
